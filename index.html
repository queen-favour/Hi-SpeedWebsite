About Us

Providing support in dire times...

We're dedicated to making borrowing and saving simple and straightforward for everyone. Everything we do at High-speed is focused on empowering you to meet your personal financial goals by providing access to a wide range of financial products, services, and educational resources, all of which are designed to help you pay less when borrowing and earn more when saving.

What We Do

Our leaders aim at empowering and supporting every Nigerian

Empowerment Loans

High-speed empowerment loan allows you to borrow money for almost any purpose, typically with a fixed term, a fixed interest rate, and a regular monthly payment plan.

Savings

Talk to one of our advisors to make a financial plan that focuses on your current needs and future goals.

Pensioners Loans

Pensioners Loan is a special scheme designed for our senior citizens to cover personal expenses such as medical treatment, travel, or any unforeseen expenses. The maximum exit age is 70 years.

Investment Plans

Get personalized investment advice. High-speed Investor is a new platform that assists you in setting, tracking, and managing your financial goals. All in one place, you can invest for the future, navigate uncertainty, and get financial tips.

Business Consultancy

We help public and private companies achieve their goals by providing business start-up advisory, SME incubation business formation, audit, risk, strategic planning, financial system design, and much more

Career opportunities
