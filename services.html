Empowerment Loans

High-speed empowerment loan allows you to borrow money for almost any purpose, typically with a fixed term, a fixed interest rate, and a regular monthly payment plan.

Savings

Pensioners Loans

Pensioners Loan is a special scheme designed for our senior citizens to cover personal expenses such as medical treatment, travel, or any unforeseen expenses. The maximum exit age is 70 years.
